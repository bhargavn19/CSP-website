1.1.9 Final project: Dawn to Dusk

For our final project in 1.1.9, we created a project that simulates the passing of one day continuously. The scene we craeted consists of a house, a few trees, and a sun moving in the sky. The sun moves across the screen until it reaches the end, and reappears on the opposite side of the screen in the appearance of a moon. The background also changes depending on what the color of the moving object is.
